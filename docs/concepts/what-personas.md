# Personas

Personas are semi-fictional profiles that represent ideal Web3 users.

These are often built using two types of data:

* Off-chain traits (like demographics and interests)
* On-chain behavior (wallet activity, transaction history)

Web3 often struggles with poor user experiences. Understanding your target personas helps you prioritize features, refine messaging, improve onboarding, and build stronger communities.
