# Audience

Are you building the future of decentralized tech? Great.

The Web3 user personas project target audience are *Web3 Builders & Designers*.

Use this research to zoom in, sharpen your focus and ship features that matter.
The Web3 Personas is a deep dive into real user types. 

Learn what drives users, how they behave, and what keeps them engaged.

Get clarity on:

* What pain points to solve?
* What are the UX must-haves?
* What are the tech expectations?
* What are the typical usage patterns in Web3?

Use these insights to craft your own persona templates^*^ - one that helps you build Web3 solutions that users *want*.  

^*^*Your template could be an informed mix of one or more Web3 user persona archetypes.*
