## Overview

The Crypto Trader/Analyst focuses on technical analysis and data-driven trading decisions.

## Characteristics

- Highly technical, often proficient in programming (Python, C++), data analysis, and machine learning, AI
- Technical analysis focused
- Risk management conscious
- Data-driven decision making
- Professional or semi-professional approach

## Behavior Patterns

- Shares charts and market analysis
- Participates in trading competitions
- Follows macro economic trends
- Uses advanced, sophisticated trading tools

## Pain Points

- Market manipulation
- Liquidity issues
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Unreliable data
- Complex workflows
- Latency

## Engagement

- Daily active trading
- Content creation

## Primary Platforms

- [TradingView](https://www.tradingview.com/)
- CEXs (e.g., [Binance](https://www.binance.com/)
- [Coinbase](https://www.coinbase.com/))
- Twitter/X
- Specialized Discord servers


## How to Target?

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-vector-difference: __Mindset__

    ---

    * Strategic not emotional, they don't feel the trades, they calculate them
    * Well-planned, disciplined, boring, consistent and control their trades
    * Attitude of a sniper, they patiently wait for the one clean shot
    * Data oriented, quick to observe divergence, fluid, adapt strategies to the market
    * They think like an analyst, not a gambler
    * Open to explore new tooling

-   :material-brain: __Motivation__

    ---

    * Staying ahead of the crowds, profit generation, market dominance
    * Ability to zoom out and see the larger picture and not just minute by minute market state change
    * Obtaining deeper insights such as adoption curves, capital flows, infrastructure 

</div>

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-rocket-launch: __Must-Have__

    ---

    * Quick ability to understand a new tool and its value proposition
    * Learning orientation: Technical Whitepapers, in-depth dev chats, tokenomics, governance proposals
    * Real time, low-latency access, high speed information and reliable data
    * Sophisticated, customizable charting tools
    * APIs for custom strategy development
    * Integration with other financial systems and databases and automated trading strategies
    * Decentralization, Anonymity

</div>
