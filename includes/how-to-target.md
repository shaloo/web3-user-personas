Target your persona by focusing on three core elements: [[why-personas#user-persona-tenets|mindset, motivation, and must-haves]]. Understanding each persona's key tenets must inform and drive your product design, website, and documentation strategy.

* Understand and leverage their mindset.
* Tap into their motivation.
* Deliver on their must-have requirements at priority.

Align with these fundamentals early on for better chances at product success and adoption.
